# Chili
## Ingredients
- 1 whole onion
- 1 whole bell pepper
- 1 jalapeno 
- 2lbs ground beef
- 1 1/2 cups beef or chicken broth
- 1 can tomato sauce 
- 28 oz can diced tomatoes 
- 1 can kidney beans
- 1 can pinto beans
- 2 tbsp tomato paste
- 2 tbsp chili powder
- 1 tbsp cumin
- 1 tbsp paprika
- 1 tbsp oregano 
- 1 tbsp salt
- 1/4 tbsp cayenne pepper 
- 1/4 tbsp black pepper

# Instructions 
1. Add olive oil to pot and heat to medium
2. Add veggies and sweat them
3. Add ground beef and spices and mix
4. Add tomato sauce, diced tomatoes, broth, and beans and let sit for 25-30 minutes on low heat, stirring occasionally
