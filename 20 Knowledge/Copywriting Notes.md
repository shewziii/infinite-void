People make decisions based on emotions. While the logical part of their minds says one thing, the emotional part is what drives those decisions. Once an emotional decision is made, our logical mind will try to justify that decision in any way it can before trying to negate that decision.

We have 4 areas that make up a person's emotional cores: 
- Desires
- Notions
- Identifications
- Characteristics

Desires are the goals that drive a person. I.E. what a person wants. Notions are beliefs about the world around the person. Identifications are the labels they give themselves. People tie a lot of their beliefs and interests to their identities.
