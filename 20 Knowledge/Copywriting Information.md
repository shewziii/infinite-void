## Landing Page & Conversion Copy

[Copywriting Statistics 2025: 91+ Stats & Insights](https://marketingltb.com/blog/statistics/copywriting-statistics/#3)

- Improving CTA copy increases conversions by **up to 30%**.
- Landing pages with a **single CTA** convert **70%** more than pages with multiple CTAs.
- Benefit-focused copy converts **20–40%** better than feature-focused copy.
- Rewriting landing pages in **voice-of-customer language** boosts conversions by **2–5X** (commonly reported).
- Removing friction words (“buy,” “sign up”) and using value words increases clicks by **14%**.
- Testimonials framed as stories increase trust by **25%**.
- Adding social proof above the fold boosts conversions by **up to 22%**.
- Bulleted benefits improve scanning and increase engagement by **12%**.
- Short landing pages often outperform long ones for **low-cost offers**.
- Long landing pages outperform short ones for **high-cost offers**.
