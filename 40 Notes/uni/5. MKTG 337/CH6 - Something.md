# The Importance of Prospecting
Prospecting is the process of locating potential customers for a product or service

- ongoing activity
- replace customers who leave
- some customers don't buy until the future or don't buy at all 
- Customers leave the territory or go bankrupt
- "Magic numbers"

--- 
# Qualifying Leads
Determining whether the lead is in fact a prospect or good candidate for a sale 

- a want or need exists
- has the ability to pay 
- authority to buy 
- can be approached favorably
- is eligible to buy
- other considerations: 
    - current contracts, etc.

--- 
# Magic Numbers 
- Leads
- Calls 
- Meetings
- Proposals
- Conversion Ratio 
- Retention

You have to know WHAT you want to accomplish before you can determine HOW

Goal: Earn $100,000 in commission my first year
Average commission: $450 
How many sales do I have to make?
223 sales = 4.3 weekly

How many prospects do I need to meet with to make 223 sales?
- My conversion rate is 35%
- 638 prospects = 12 weekly 

How many leads do I need to contact to make 223 sales?
- My lead to prospect conversion ratio is 5%

# Finding Prospects 
- Satisfied Customers: current and previous customers
- Endless chain: one additional lead from each person you encounter/interview
- Networking: use personal relationships or relationships with other to connect with new leads
- Center of Influence: cultivate well-know, influential relationships in your territory with people who are willing to introduce you to others
- Social Media: use online tools such as linkedIn, Facebook, IG, Twitter to prospect for new customers and maintain contact with existing
- CRM databases: mine and work old leads
- Shows, fairs, community events
- Inside salespeople: if anyone inside your company that can generate leads, service department, etc.
- Other sources
- Marketing department
- Cold calling

---
# Planning the Sales Call 
Planning allows you to be efficient in your time each day and accomplish more in your role

1. Gather information about the prospect and firm 
2. Set your call objectives
3. Make an appointment or leave a voicemail
4. Follow-up

Planning is the most important step in the process, however, know the balance or spending too much time

# Common things buyers value 
- on-time delivery
- products exact to specifications
- competitive pricing 
- proper packaging
- technical support/service 
- level of technological innovation 
- good emergency response

# Leaving Effective Voicemails
- Voicemails allow you to leave more quality and personalized messages than emails 
    - You should engage in both 
- Voicemails have a 4-6% response rate 
    - You can increase this by being effective 
- More than 44% of sales professionals stop after 1st attempt 
    - Requires greater than 8 attempts to gain a leads attention
