Throughout my career, I've heard just about every explanation for why people buy. Some marketers believe its solely to fulfill a need. Some dive deep into the psychology, looking at how histories, personalities, and motivations influence decision-making.

In Martin Lindstrom's "Buyology: Truth and Lies About Why We Buy", he takes a look at why market research can sometimes fail to uncover the motivations behind purchase decisions.

In this post, I'll be taking you through some of the ideas and key takeaways from this book.
