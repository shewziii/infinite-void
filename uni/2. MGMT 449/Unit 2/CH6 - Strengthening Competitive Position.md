# Blue Ocean Strategy
Offers growth in revenues and profit by discovering or inventing new industry segments that create an altogehter new demand.

Called that because if you do something new, there is nobody around in the area. Just like discovering a new ocean.

# Ways to Prevent Competitors Attacking Your Market Share 
- Stay ahead with technology
- Introduce new features regularly
- Maintain economy pricing
- Discourage buyers from buying competitor brands

# Timing
Because of first-mover advantages and disadvantages, competitive advantages can spring from **when** a move is made as well as form **what** move is made

---
# First Mover Advantages 
1. Creates strong brand loyalty
2. Switching costs
3. Property protection rights 
4. Enables movement down the learning curve ahead of rivals
5. First movers can set the industry standard

---
# Late-mover Advantages
1. Being first can be more expensive, moving in later can give more experience
2. Uncertainty of industry 
3. First mover loyalty can be low, that's when yo move in to take that advantage

# Backward integration
- Moving closer to the supplier

- Advantages
    - Better economies of scale 
    - Reduction of supplier power 
    - More efficient
    - Reduction in costs of major inputs

# Forward Integration 
- Moving closer to the buyer

- Advantages 
    - lower overall costs be increasing channel activity efficiencies relative to competitors 
    - Increase bargaining power through control of channel activities 
    - Gain better access to end users 
    - Strenghten and reinforce brand awareness

--- 
# Outsource when an activity
- something can be don better or less expensively by specialists
- not crucial to sustainable competitive advantages 
- improves flexibility and speeds time to market 
- Reduces risk due to new technology or buyer preferences
- Allows concentration on core businesses, leverages key resources, and is more successful outsourced

---
# Risk of Outsourcing 
- loss of control over quality 
- patented products are at risk 
- less resources and capabilities needed to be a master of its own destiny
