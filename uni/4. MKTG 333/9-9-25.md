# E-Commerce vs E-Business 
E-Commerce 
- Use of internet to transact business 
    - includes web, mobile browsers and apps
- More formally:
    - digitally enabled commercial transactions between and among organizations and individuals

E-Business
- Digital enabling of transactions and processes **within** a firm, involving information systems under firm's control
- Does not include commercial transactions involving an exchange of value across organizational values

# 8 Unique Features of E-Commerce Technology 
1. Ubiquity
	Available almost everywhere at any time
2. Universal Standards
	Standards that are shared by every nation in the world
3. Richness
	Refers to the complexity and content of a message
4. Interactivity
	Two way communication between merchant and the consumer
5. Information Density
	The total amount of information to all market participants
6. Personalization and Customization
	The ability to change the service or product for individual needs or preferences, and targeting individuals  based on name, interests, and past purchases
7. Social Technology
	User generated content and social media
8. Global Reach
	Technology is available all around the world
