# Shoppers: Browsers and Buyers
- Shoppers: over 90% of internet users
    - 80% are buyers
    - 13% are browsers
- Online research before shopping in store
- Online traffic also influenced by offline brands and shopping
- E-commerce and traditional commerce are coupled: Part of a continuum of consuming behaviour

---
# Customer Decision Making Process

**Awareness/needs recognition**
Targeted Display ads, email ads, and social media

**Search**
Search engines, online catalogs, site visits, targeted email, social networks

**Evaluation of Alternatives**
Search engines, online catalogs, site visits, product reviews, user evaluations, social networks 

**Purchase**
Online promotions, discounts, targeted email, flash sales

**Post-Purchase Behavior/loyalty**
Communities of consumption, newsletters, customer email, online updates, social networks

---
# How Consumers Shop
- How shoppers find online vendors
    - highly intentional, goal-oriented
    - Search engines
    - Marketplaces
    - Specific retail site

- About 8% of Internet users don't shop online
    - Trust factors
    - Hassle factors

---
# Two Most Important Factors Shaping Decision to Purchase Online
**Utility**
Better prices, convenience, speed

**Trust**
Perception of credibility, ease of use, perceived risk.
Sellers develop trust by building strong reputations for honesty, fairness, delivery

# Customer Journey
- The process a customer or prospect goes through to achive a goal with a company
    - Needs 
    - Motivations
    - Pain points

# Online Marketing Metrics
- Audience size/market share metrics
    - impressions, CTR, page views, viewability rate, stickiness, loyalty, reach, recency
- Conversion to customer metrics
    - acquisition rate, conversion rate, browse to buy
- Video ad metrics
    - Views, retention rate
- E-mail campaign metrics
