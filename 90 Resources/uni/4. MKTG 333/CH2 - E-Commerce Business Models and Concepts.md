# Value Proposition
"Why should the customer buy from us?"

Successful e-commerce value propositions:
- Personalization/customization
- Reduction of product search, price discovery costs
- Facilitation of transactions by managing product delivery

# Revenue Model
"How will you make money?"

Major types of revenue model:
- Advertising revenue model
    - Making money through advertising 
- Subscription model
- Freemium Strategy

# Market Opportunity
"What marketspace do you intend to serve and what is its size?"

Typically divided into smaller niches

# Competitive Environment
"Who else occupies your intended marketspace?"

Other companies selling similar products in the same marketspace
Includes both direct and indirect competitors 

Influenced by:
- Number and size of active competitors 
- Each competitor's market share 
- Competitor's profitability

# Competitive Advantage
"What special advantages does your firm bring to the marketspace?"

# Market Strategy 
"How do you plan to promote your prducts or services to attract your target audience?"
- Details how a company intends to enter market and attract customers
